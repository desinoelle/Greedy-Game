How to Play

Object

Be the first player to reach 10,000 points. When one player hits 10,000, the other gets one final turn to beat their score.

Getting On the Board

You must score at least 500 points in a single turn before you can start banking points.

Your Turn

  1. Roll all six dice.
  2. Select at least one scoring die.
  3. Choose to roll again with the remaining dice, or end your turn to bank your points.
  4. If you roll and score nothing, you lose all unbanked points and your turn ends.
  5. If all six dice score, pick them all up and keep rolling!
